## 2024-04-25 - [OpenAIAdapter bubbles up APIConnectionError] **Edge Case:** [Mocked the OpenAI client to raise an APIConnectionError when the external API is unreachable or times out] **Learning:** [The adapter does not mask or swallow network connection failures; it bubbles them up to the caller] **Action:** [Ensure retry or fallback logic is handled at a higher orchestration layer rather than within the adapter itself]
