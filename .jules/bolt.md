## 2024-05-06 - Caching LLM Provider Factories **Learning:** Async LLM clients (like `AsyncOpenAI` or `AsyncAnthropic`) carry heavy initialization overhead. Their instances, or the factory functions creating them, should be cached to prevent performance degradation during repetitive processing. **Action:** Apply `@functools.cache` to globally accessed factory functions (`get_llm_provider`), and ensure test isolation by adding an `autouse=True` fixture in `conftest.py` that calls `<function_name>.cache_clear()`.
